Wash colocasia leaves and wipe them dry. Wipe the leaves with the dry cloth.

In a mixing bowl, take green chillies,Ginger paste,Garlic paste,red chilli powder,turmeric powder,black pepper powder,cinnamon powder,cumin powder,asafoetida (Hing),coriander seeds powder (dhana powder),Sesame Seeds,tbsp Tamarind pulp,Gram flour (Besan),chooped curry leaves,tbsp jaggery,Grated fresh coconut
rice flour,Salt as per taste,water as required. Mix well to make a thick batter.

The batter should be thick.
Spread the leaves on a plain surface with the darker side down.
Now spread the batter on frount-side of the leaf and place the next leaf on top of it and apply another layer of besan batter on it as well.
Continue this process with 10 to 12 leaves.
Roll the edges vertically from up side-down and spread a little batter on the end of the roll.
Now tie the roll tightly with thread or set it in container and a side for 3 to 5 mintues.

Now heat a non steak cooking pot(can use normal cooking pot) and add oil and heat it untiel it hot and then add mustards seeds and let it crak and burst .now add sesame seeds and turn of the flame .Now sliced the roll (minimum 2cm to maximum 3cm).Now make 2 layers first of raw sooked peanut and then sliced sooked potato now layer it with slices of leaves(aluwadi)and continue with another 2 layer of sooked raw peanut and sliced potato again.Now cover the pot with lid and turn on the flame and cook aluwadi for 10 minutes at high flame and flip the aluwadi and cook again for 15 minutes at low flame(if aluwadi is sticky or having milk still then open the lid and cook aluwadi untill milk get completely absorb and golden brown.)(while flipping the aluwadi if the aluwadi get broke or crakes no problem aluwadi can be eaten broken )
NOW COCONUT MILK STEAMED ALUWADI IS READY TO SERVE.
